Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a reservation?

You can make a reservation by filling out the booking form on our website or by calling our customer service team. Once we receive your request, our team will contact you to confirm availability and finalize the booking details.

What is included in the villa rental?

Our villa rentals include accommodation, access to all amenities listed for each property, and basic utilities. Additional services such as private chef, transportation, and guided tours can be arranged at an additional cost.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer a flexible cancellation policy. For cancellations made 7 days before check-in, you receive a full refund. For cancellations made 3-6 days before check-in, you receive a 50% refund. Cancellations made less than 3 days before check-in are non-refundable.

Are pets allowed in the villas?

Some of our villas are pet-friendly. Please check the property details or contact our customer service team to confirm if pets are allowed at your chosen villa. Additional charges may apply for pet accommodation.

Is transportation provided from airports or stations?

We can arrange airport or station transfers at an additional cost. Please let us know your travel details at the time of booking, and our team will coordinate the best transportation options for you.
